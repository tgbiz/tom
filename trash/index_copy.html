ABOUT ME

As an EDI Specialist I developed scripts that automated the secure uploading of encrypted medical claims and the downloading of report files. My scripts simplified the process, increasing operational efficiency. Members of the EDI team adopted them, utilizing them as their own.

After a short stint as an applications system administrator in 2013, I saw an opportunity and changed direction, taking an educational sabbatical to become a software developer.

In 2015 I interviewed for a developer job with Hewlett-Packard and was hired. Since then I have been growing skills. The learning never stops.

Other interests: My wife, DSLR photography, video editing, investing, the outdoors, hiking, reading, the gym, piano, singing bass in church choir, amateur theology, and my dog.
